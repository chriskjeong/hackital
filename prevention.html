Online Harassment Prevention

help others, help yourself



Mob Mentality: When someone begins to harass another online, it opens the doors for others to see the behavior as acceptable. As a result, you can get an avalanche where many more negative comments begin to pile on the thread.
Doxing: To maliciously search for and publish private or identifying information about a particular individual on the internet.
Anonymity: When a user is banned for abuse on a platform, and simply creates a brand new account to continue the abuse from.
